### How do I Mint?

1. First **Connect** an Ethereum wallet! Don't have a wallet? We recommend Meta Mask
    - Browser extensions will automatically be deteced, and a request will be sent with your provider.
    - When using a mobile device: Use the built in browser within your wallet's app.

2. Next, **Select** up to NINE Frogs to mint per transaction!

3. Then, Click on **Mint** (transact) to send to submit the transaction!

4. Finally, Review and **Confirm** the request with your provider and send the transaction! 

- #### What are gas fees?
    - A gas fee is required when minting any NFT on to the Ethereum blockchain!

### How do I view my Frogs?

- View your Frogs at [freshfrogs.io/the-pad](https://freshfrogs.io/the-pad)

- #### My Frogs are not displaying?
    - Our website integreated OpenSea's API; Sometimes this can fall behind.
    - Select '**Check by Block**' to manually check your Frogs on the blockchain
