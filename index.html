About Fresh Frogs

Fresh Frogs is an Ethereum NFT collection of 4,040 hand-crafted pixel frogs, each with unique body colors, eyes, accessories, and backgrounds. Holders can stake their frogs in the Pond to earn $FLYZ — the longer you stake, the higher your level (Level 1 → 2 → 3). You can also explore the rarity rankings to find the rarest frogs in the collection.
