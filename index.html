About Fresh Frogs NFT

🐸 The Collection

Fresh Frogs NFT is a collection of 4,040 randomly generated frogs on Ethereum, each unique with thousands of trait combinations. Stake to earn rewards, level up, unlock animated traits, and morph two frogs into one.

🌿 Our Mission

Over 200,000 acres of rainforest are burned every day. A portion of every sale is donated to The Rainforest Foundation to help protect the natural habitat of real frogs around the world.
